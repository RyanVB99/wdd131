Project Site Plan

Site Name

Per Te

Per Te is Italian for "For You". This may or may not be the final name as it is not very descriptive, but I want a name that is simple and interesting.

I like how "Per Te" sounds, I like the meaning as I enjoy cooking "for" people, and it shares a name with a song I have loved most of my life.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to showcase meals I have created and curate recipes I have made or want to make.

Scenarios

  • What are some elegant recipes I can make?
  • I want to see examples of upscale multi-course meals.

Color Schema

I'm not sure yet what colors I am going to use. Currently, I'm leaning towards the colors you see here (white, beige, brown and black). They don't quite 'feel' the way I want, but they get the job done.

  • Background Color - EDE0D4
  • Font Color - 2E2B26
  • Accent Color - 8C7A77
  • Secondary Color - E5C49B

Typography

  • Headings will use the Italiana font
  • Everything else will use Roboto
